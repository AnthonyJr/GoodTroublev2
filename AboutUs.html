Mission Statement

Good Trouble as a non-profit orginization that aspires to inspire the younger generation. We got our name from a quote from John Lewis, an american policitian and civil rights leader. He said, " You must find a way to get in good trouble, necessary trouble. You must help change America, you must help change the world. With your degree, you are prepared to go out there and speak up and speak out". YOU CAN BE THE CHANGE YOU WANT TO SEE IN THE WORLD! With good trouble, we desire to teach, inspire, and influence.
